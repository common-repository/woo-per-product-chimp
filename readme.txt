Manage your Mailchimp inside WordPress. 

== Description ==

This WooCommerce plugin addon let's you easily assign your customers to your specificied MailChimp list based on the products that they purchase from you. This plugin is the perfect tool when you need to send newsletters, product updates, sales announcements, new product(s) announcements, discount coupons, or whatever to the right customers, at the right time, EVERYTIME on AUTOPILOT.

Savvy marketers know that list segmentation is the winning strategy to drastically improve email marketing campaigns and boost sales.

Segmenting your customers depending on their purchase history means you can effectively tailor the content of your emails to each specific group, thus matching the right customers with the right offers.

Now our revolutionary new plugin allows you to INSTANTLY add customers to whatever list you want inside your MailChimp newsletter account the moment they make a purchase on your site.

No more fiddling about inside MailChimp wasting time manually adding customers to lists. This plugin automatically does it for you!

Yep, that’s right! Now you can laser target the right customers with the right offers, updates, promotions and anything else you can think of with this awesome groundbreaking plugin that we’ve specially developed called WooPPChimp.

List segmentation made easy, hassle free and immediate!

Get your customers on the right list…and boost your profits NOW.


== Installation ==

1. Upload WooCommerce Per Product Mailchimp to the /wp-content/plugins/ directory
2. Activate the plugin through the Plugins menu in WordPress
